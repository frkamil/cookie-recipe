Chocolate Chip Cookies

Learn how to make the best chocolate chip cookies advertised by allrecipes.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • Butter - softened
  • Suger
  • Eggs - room temperature
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • Water - hot
  • Salt
  • Flour - all purpose
  • Chocolate chips - semisweet

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Add eggs (one at a time, and then stir in vanilla).
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter with salt.
  5. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for ten minutes until edges are nicely browned.
  8. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
